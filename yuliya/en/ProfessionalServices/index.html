Landscape Architecture and Design

I am a landscape architect graduated from the Montreal University. I currently live and work in Chelsea, province of Quebec.

My professional expertise is based on planning and designing landscape architecture projects of all scales and sizes at commercial, municipal and residential levels, including urban projects, residential development, project preparation and supervision of construction sites.

Professional Services:

  • Consultation for landscape design;
  • Conceptual free hand drawings;
  • Choice of plants and other diverse materials to use in your project;
  • Suggestion concerning the implantation of structures;

Whether they are small town gardens, terraces, country estates or residential projects I like the diversity in my work. Each project I create is custom-designed and planned to fit each individual client.

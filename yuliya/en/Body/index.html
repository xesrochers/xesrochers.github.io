Eyebrow Restoration

You may already know me as a professional seascape artist, but did you know that I’m also a certified permanent makeup artist?

I’m proud to have received my certification from the International Permanent Makeup Academy in Montreal. I offer a semi-permanent eyebrow restoration service as well as eyeliner and intralash enhancements—helping you achieve flawless results with a natural, lasting finish.

Imagine waking up every day with perfectly shaped brows and defined eyes—no more time wasted filling them in!

 

Semi-Permanent Eyebrow Dermapigmentation

During the procedure, cosmetic pigment is delicately applied in fine strokes that mimic natural hair.

This is not decorative makeup—it’s a true reconstruction of your eyebrows, designed to seamlessly blend with your own hair. The result? Beautiful, natural-looking brows that appear effortlessly your own.

Why choose semi-permanent eyebrow restoration?

  • Save time every day by eliminating the need for pencils, powders, or gels.
  • Enhance and preserve your natural brow shape without sacrificing fullness or definition.
  • Restore symmetry and fullness if your brows are sparse or uneven.
  • Results before and after the procedure of eyebrow restoration
 
 

Eyeliner & Intralash, Semi-Permanent Dermapigmentation

The eyeliner procedure is a refined and precise technique that enhances the eyes by delicately implanting pigment along the lash line. Performed with the utmost care, it goes beyond a simple treatment—it is an elegant enhancement that defines and elevates the natural beauty of your eyes.

Main styles:

  • Intralash (Lash Enhancement): A subtle technique where pigment is placed between the lashes to create the illusion of fuller, darker lashes—without a visible line.
  • Eyeliner: A more defined line above the lashes, customized to your eye shape and preferences—soft and natural or slightly bolder. This option always includes intralash.
  • Shaded Eyeliner (Smokey or Eyeshadow Effect): A technique that mimics the look of soft eyeshadow or diffused eyeliner. It enhances the eyes with a more dramatic shaded effect on the lids and includes intralash.

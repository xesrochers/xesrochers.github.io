Eyebrow Restoration

You already know me as a painter, but I'm also a certified permanent makeup artist specialized in nano eyebrows! I offer an eyebrow restoration service using a demograph machine.

During the procedure, cosmetic pigment is placed into the skin in strokes that mimic hair. This is NOT DECORATIVE MAKEUP, but rather a true reconstruction of the natural appearance of your eyebrows. My design will 'merge' with your own hair and no one will assume the artist's intervention.

Advantages of the procedure:

  • Simplifies your daily routine
  • Approach that preserves your existing hair
  • This restoration method is also suitable for people suffering from eyebrow alopecia, as the treatment is gentle on the skin with a natural result

So, if you no longer want to pencil your eyebrows to achieve the right shape or if your eyebrows are asymmetrical and you want to make them look fuller... Why not try semi-permanent restoration?

Contact me for further information, I'll be happy to assist you.

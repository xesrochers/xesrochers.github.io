A garden is a success when we feel a harmony between the structure and its owners. It is an outdoor living space with its proportions, colors and shapes.

I am a landscape architect graduated from the Montreal University. I currently live and work in Gatineau.

My professional expertise is based on planning and designing landscape architecture projects of all scales and sizes at commercial, municipal and residential levels, including urban projects, residential development, project preparation and supervision of construction sites.

Professional Services:
    Consultation for site design and conception;

    Design development including planning, preparation of contract documents, presentation;

    Choice of plants and other diverse materials to use in your project;

    Suggestion concerning the structure implantation;

    Conceptual design sketches and technical drawings;

    3D conceptual drawings.

Additional Service:
    Construction project site supervision.
Conduct of the project:
  • First meeting: identification of the needs, orientation and project’s steps to meet your specifications; at this stage, you can choose to phase the project or execute at once; you are free to choose detail level for your project: free hand sketches with ideas, 3D design drawings, complete master-plan or technical details of structural element (patio, pergola, pool etc.);

  • Second meeting: presentation of the preliminary design sketch and discussion concerning the changes to be made, if necessary;

  • Third meeting: presentation of the final development plan. You are free to select the contractors that are most competitively priced and best equipped to build your project.

Whether they are small town gardens, terraces, country estates or residential projects, I like the diversity in my work. Each project I create is custom-designed and meticulously planned to fit each individual client. The design concepts that drive my style are progressive, refreshing, current and full of integrity.

The master plan made by a landscape architect is the first stone laid in the foundation of a successful garden.

I present you Water in oil and pastel. My love for The Sea feeds all of my seascapes. Oils are painted on linen and cotton canvas as its texture complements the image. Pastels are laying on a famous French pastel mat. The power of the waves is my favourite subject... The particular technique used in Russian School of Arts creates transparency, intensity and depth of the water in all its states and moods. Oil creates texture which seems to jet out of the canvas giving you desire to touch the painting. Meanwhile, pastel pure pigments give a very light texture to an image…
